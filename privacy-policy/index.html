Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in PopFrame for iOS.

We Collect No Personal Information Using Our Applications

We do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in PopFrame for iOS.

Individual settings relating to PopFrame are not personal and are stored only on your device. You might also be asked to provide access to your photo library, but this is only so you can save your exports to your library. We don’t process that information at all and have no access to it.

Our Application Complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act

Our application doesn’t collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to get in touch with us at hello@popframe.app
